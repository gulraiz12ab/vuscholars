About Virtual University of Pakistan

The Virtual University of Pakistan (VU) is the country's first HEC-recognized public university built entirely on modern Information & Communication Technologies. Established in 2002 by the Government of Pakistan, VU offers affordable, world-class education through satellite TV, the Internet, and its advanced Learning Management System (VULMS). With over 200 campuses nationwide and 500,000+ enrolled students, VU is Pakistan's largest distance-learning institution.

VU degrees are fully recognized by HEC and hold the highest 'W' category ranking. Admissions open twice a year â€” Spring (January/February) and Fall (August/September). No entry test required for undergraduate programs.
